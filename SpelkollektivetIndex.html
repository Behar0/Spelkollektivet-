Spelkollektivet

The Indie Quest is a unique course blending programming, art, and production, tailored for indie game creation. Dive straight into practical learning through game projects instead of isolated subjects. Start as an apprentice, mastering coding through missions, then progress to advanced concepts while crafting your own games. Manage projects effectively to prototype and polish your creations for sharing. Choose any visual style for your games, with personalized study plans covering art fundamentals and technical aspects. Whether it's pixel art, 3D, traditional drawing, or digital painting, we've got you covered. Explore the curriculum for more details.

In autumn 2020, Spelkollektivet started its adult education in game development, The Indie Quest. It is a komvux training that focuses on getting the students ready to start their own indie game projects and goes through the basics of both programming and graphics.
